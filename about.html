Editor's Letter

I’m Hana-Lisa. At 21, I navigate the space between rigorous academia and creative intuition. Currently pursuing a Master’s in Media, Culture & Society at Pantheon-Assas, my background in literature at Sorbonne shapes my approach: storytelling with clarity and critical insight.

My journey into journalism revealed a tension between the artistic expression I value and the neutral style of mainstream press. Experiences at Elle Magazine and La Tribune solidified my interest in economic reporting but also my refusal to compromise on style.

I am building an independent approach, where writing remains vibrant, meaningful, and unapologetically intellectual.

“I could feel that Notre-Dame, Flaubert or Truffaut were familiar to me... they became my heritage.”
