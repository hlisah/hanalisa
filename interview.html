A Mother’s Tale: From Seoul to Boston

An intimate account of a mother navigating life, culture, and family across continents.

Moving from Seoul to Boston was not only a physical journey but an emotional and cultural one. As a mother, adapting to a new society while preserving one's heritage presents both challenges and insights.

« Every culture carries its own rhythm; understanding it shapes how we live and communicate. »

Adapting to a New Environment

The transition demanded learning new languages, educational systems, and social norms. It taught resilience and the subtle art of balancing tradition with modernity.

Parenting Across Cultures

Raising children in a different culture highlights the contrasts and similarities in family dynamics. It brings new perspectives on education, identity, and personal growth.

Reflections on Media and Storytelling

Sharing one’s story through writing, interviews, or social platforms bridges the personal with the universal. Media becomes a tool to transmit cultural memory and lived experience.

« Stories are the threads that connect past, present, and future. »

This journey demonstrates that migration is not only a change of place but a transformation of perspective, enriching both personal life and collective understanding.
